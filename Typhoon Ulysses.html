Typhoon Ulysses:

Typhoon Ulysses first saw/landfall near midnight on november 11in the Quezon province, typhoon Ulysses is one of the strongest typhoons in the philippines especially in the part of central luzon. this typhoon brought many people into danger or death

How do typhoon ulysses start

On 08 November 2020, Tropical Depression “Ulysses” entered the Philippine Area of Responsibility (PAR) and became a Tropical Storm on 10 November 2020 as it continued to move northwestward

Impact to every person

This typhoon ulysses has big impact to everyone especially the people in the central luzon people need to gathered in one place in this time of pandemic Many infrastructures were damaged/destroyed and some died.

What to do when there a typhoon

  • Listen to the radio or TV for information
  • Secure your home, close storm shutters and secure outdoor objects or bring them indoors.
  • Turn off the main switch for utilities like water and electricity.
  • Have a flashlight or lamp in hand in case of emergency and loss of electricity.
  • Keep away from possible dangers like glass windows.

What should we not do during typhoon

  • Avoid using running water.
  • Do not touch concrete structures.
  • ry not to light candles.
  • Avoid hiding in your basement.
  • Avoid standing near windows.
